Shipping & Delivery

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days for domestic orders. Express shipping options are available at checkout for delivery within 1-2 business days. International delivery times vary by location but typically take 7-14 business days.

What are your shipping rates?

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $100. For orders under $100, standard shipping is $7.99. Express shipping is available for $19.99 regardless of order value. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on destination.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Please note that customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or import fees that may apply in their country.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and link to track your package. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused products in their original packaging with proof of purchase. Return shipping is free for domestic returns. Some products may have specific return conditions, which will be noted on the product page.

How long does it take to process a refund?

Once we receive your returned item, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment method and financial institution, but typically takes 5-10 business days.

Can I exchange a product?

Yes, we offer exchanges for a different size, color, or product. To initiate an exchange, please contact our customer service team or start a return from your account and select the exchange option. Exchanges are subject to product availability.

Products & Warranty

What warranty do your products come with?

Most of our products come with a manufacturer's warranty ranging from 1 to 3 years. The specific warranty period and terms are listed on each product page. Our premium Elite products come with an extended 3-year warranty that includes accidental damage protection.

Are your products authentic and new?

Yes, all products sold on EliteStore are 100% authentic, brand new, and sourced directly from manufacturers or authorized distributors. We do not sell refurbished, used, or counterfeit products.

Do you offer product customization?

We offer limited customization options on select products, such as engraving or custom configurations. These options will be available during checkout if applicable to the product. For bulk orders or special requests, please contact our corporate sales team.

Account & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shop Pay. We also offer financing options through Affirm for qualifying orders over $250.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your personal and payment information. We are PCI DSS compliant and never store your complete payment details on our servers. For added security, we recommend using PayPal or Apple Pay.

How do I create an account?

You can create an account during checkout or by clicking the "Account" icon in the header. Creating an account allows you to track orders, save your payment methods for faster checkout, create wishlists, and receive exclusive offers.

Technical Support

How do I get technical support for my product?

We offer comprehensive technical support for all products purchased from EliteStore. You can contact our support team via phone at 1-800-ELITE-HELP, email at support@elitestore.com, or live chat during business hours. Many products also include dedicated manufacturer support.

Where can I find product manuals and documentation?

Product manuals, specifications, and documentation are available on each product page under the "Downloads" section. You can also access these materials from your account if you've purchased the product. If you need additional documentation, please contact our support team.

Do you offer setup assistance?

Yes, we offer complimentary basic setup guidance for all products. For complex installations, we offer premium setup services at an additional cost. Please contact our support team to learn more about our setup assistance options.

Still have questions?

Our customer support team is here to help you with any additional questions or concerns you may have.
