Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to questions you may have. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly.

How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery is available for an additional fee and takes 1-2 business days. International delivery may take 7-14 business days depending on the destination.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused products in their original packaging. Returns are free for customers in the United States. International returns may be subject to shipping fees.
Do you offer technical support?
Yes, we offer comprehensive technical support for all our products. Our support team is available 24/7 via phone, email, and live chat. You can also visit our support center for troubleshooting guides and tutorials.
Can I change or cancel my order?
You can change or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it. After that, orders enter our processing system and cannot be modified. If you need to make changes after this window, please contact our customer service team immediately.
